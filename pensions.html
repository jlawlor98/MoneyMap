1. Pension Types Demystified

🤝 Workplace Pension

💼 Employer contributions
⚡ Auto-enrolment
💡 Minimum 8% total

🎯 SIPP (Self-Invested)

📈 Full control
💷 Wider investment choice
💡 £1k+ annual fees

2. Contribution Strategies

🚀 Starter (25-35)

💷 10% of income
📈 Focus: Growth funds
🎯 Goal: £100k by 40

📊 Accelerator (35-50)

💷 15-20% of income
📈 Mix: Growth + income
🎯 Goal: £300k by 50

  • 🏦 Annuity: Guaranteed income
  • 📈 Drawdown: Flexible access
  • 💷 Lump Sum: 25% tax-free

4. Common Mistakes

  • ⏳ Starting too late
  • 📉 Not reviewing investments
  • 💸 Paying high fees
  • ✅ Solution: Regular reviews
